Renault Car Models

Renault car price starts at Rs 5.49 Lakh for the cheapest model which is Kwid and the price of most expensive model, which is Kiger starts at Rs 7.03 Lakh. Renault offers 3 car models in India, including 1 car in Hatchback category, 1 car in Compact SUV category, 1 car in MUV category.

French automobile brand, Renault made its debut in the country back in 2005. The company has its headquarters in Chennai while the brand’s factory is located in Oragadam.
Renault India currently sells four models in India including the Kwid, Triber, Duster, and Kiger. The Duster is one of the oldest models from the company, which was originally launched in 2012. The carmaker introduced the Kwid hatchback in 2015, followed by the Triber in 2019. The Kiger is its latest offering and it made its debut in the Indian market in February this year.
Renault’s plant in Oragadam has a capacity to produce 4.80 lakh vehicles per annum. The company has a presence of more than 500 sales and service outlets across India, including more than 200 workshop-on-wheels locations. The French brand has also launched a rural float mobile showroom in the country.
