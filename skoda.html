Skoda Auto India is a subsidiary of Czech automobile manufacturer Skoda Auto. The company is a part of Volkswagen Group India and currently heads the Skoda Auto Volkswagen India Privte Limited (SAVWIPL). The Indian subsidiary of Skoda Auto was founded in 2001 and has its manufacturing facility located in Aurangabad, Maharashtra and a research facility in Pune, Maharashtra.
Skoda under its India 2.0 project has restructured its plans in the country. The product portfolio of Skoda India currently includes the Octavia, Superb, and Kushaq. In 2021, Skoda introduced the new-generation Octavia followed by the all-new Kushaq that marked the company’s entry into the mid-size SUV segment.
The Czech carmaker has discontinued to Rapid sedan and will replace it with the Slavia in early 2022. Further to be added in the portfolio is the Kodiaq facelift that will be introduced in January 2022
